Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers to common questions from students and parents.

Do you teach one-to-one or groups?

Both options are possible. One-to-one classes are ideal for focused improvement, while small groups can be arranged when suitable.

Which countries can join?

Students from any country can join online classes as long as the timing is manageable for both sides.

Can timings be adjusted?

Yes. Schedule flexibility is available depending on subject, student level, and advance coordination.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes. A trial class can be arranged so students and parents can understand the teaching style before regular sessions begin.

Which class levels do you support?

I support different school levels depending on the subject. The exact level can be discussed during registration.

How are classes conducted?

Classes are conducted online with guided explanation, practice, and direct student interaction.
